Why Use a Strong Password?

A strong password is your first defense against unauthorized access to your accounts. It keeps your personal information secure and reduces the risk of identity theft and cyberattacks. Here are some tips for a secure password:

  • Include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Avoid using personal information like your name or birthdate.
  • Keep it at least 12 characters long.
  • Use different passwords for different accounts.
